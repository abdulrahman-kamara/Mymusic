As a child, like many children,
Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu was fascinated by superhero comics.
He wanted to be his own superhero,
so he named himself Burna Boy,
a moniker that has followed him into a career as one of the defining musical acts of today’s
African diaspora.
Last November, after his fourth studio album African Giant permeated the summer from Abuja to Brooklyn,
he sold out London’s Wembley Arena. Now, just a year after African Giant,
Burna returns with Twice as Tall, and with it,
a more resonant origin story—one that
explains his ascent from 2018’s breakthrough single “Ye.”
